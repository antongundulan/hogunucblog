Sid Sriram is a talented Indian-American musician, known for his exceptional skills in Carnatic music and playback singing. With a mesmerizing voice and captivating performances, he has won the hearts of music lovers worldwide. Let’s dive into the details of his life and career.

Birth and Family

Sid Sriram was born on 19th May 1990 in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. He hails from a musically inclined family, with his mother, Latha Sriram, being a music teacher and his first music teacher as well. Sid’s father was a serviceman, but specific details about him remain unknown. He moved to California with his parents when he was just one year old.

Education and Debut

Sid Sriram pursued his passion for music by learning Carnatic music from his mother, Latha Sriram. He further honed his skills by graduating in Music Production and Engineering from Brekelle College Of Music. His debut as a playback singer was in the song “Ennodu Nee Irunthaal.”

Career and Net Worth

Sid Sriram is a multitalented musician, songwriter, and playback singer. Inspired by the legendary Indian musician A R Rahman, Sid has made a mark in the Tamil and Telugu music industries. He is known for singing popular songs like “Alladhe Siragiye,” “Raajali,” and “Macho.” One of his notable achievements is the song “Adiye,” which was composed by A R Rahman.
