Martha Stewart Posted an 81st Birthday Selfie Thats the Definition of GlowySee Pic

Publish date: 2024-08-08

Martha Stewart is forever living her best life. Whether it shows her playfully posing up a storm on the Strip in Las Vegas, gracing the red carpet, or whipping up delicious meals to serve al fresco in aspirational style, her Instagram feed is a thing of joy. She also knows how to turn the camera on herself: The original food-fluencer posted a radiant selfie on Wednesday, August 3 to celebrate her 81st birthday.

As she was lying down, her short blonde bob falling away from her face, Stewart’s skin glowed with health in the shot, which she took after a “delicious lunch” spent drinking wine by Australian brand 19 Crimes. Martha Stewart complemented her enviably smooth and plump skin with an ultra natural makeup look.

A subtle bronze flush over cheeks and brown eyeliner to define her eyes (a makeup artist tip: Swap black for brown as skin matures; it’s more forgiving), the look was topped off by a touch of lip balm. If this is what 81 looks like, the future is looking bright.

This post was originally published on British Vogue.
