James Middleton Is Engaged to Girlfriend Alizee Thevenet

Publish date: 2024-08-19
  • Kate Middleton's brother, James Middleton, is engaged to his girlfriend, Alizee Thevenet.
  • James confirmed the happy news on Instagram with a photo of Thevenet's engagement ring.
  • James went Instagram official with Thevenet in May of this year when he shared a sweet sailing selfie with his beau. The Duchess of Cambridge's brother also took his now-fiancé to Wimbledon this summer.

James Middleton is officially engaged to be married.

Pippa Middleton and the Duchess of Cambridge's brother confirmed via Instagram on Sunday that he'd proposed to his girlfriend Alizee Thevenet. The adorable couple took their relationship public this summer, and attended Wimbledon with the Middleton family.

The Daily Mail previously reported via one of James's friends, "They’ve got engaged. Alizee has been wearing a beautiful sapphire sparkler."

Apparently James was keeping the happy news low-key, but friends and family are getting excited about the prospect of another Middleton wedding. Per Daily Mail's source, "They’ve been keeping the engagement secret, but news has started to leak out among their friends. They’ll be announcing it formally next week."

A day after the news broke, James took to Instagram to confirm, "She said OUI ❤️. Our secret is out but we couldn’t be happier to share the news 🥂🍾 #jalizee 🇬🇧🇫🇷." He also shared the sweetest selfie with his beau, which revealed her gorgeous, sparkly ring.

In January 2019, James wrote an op-ed for the Daily Mail about living with depression. He explained, "Depression is only a small part of the complex jigsaw that is me. Since childhood, I’ve known I was severely dyslexic—both letters and numbers still jump and blur on the page in front of me and some days I have difficulty spelling even the simplest words. But it was only when, a year ago, I was also diagnosed with Attention Deficit Disorder (ADD) that all the quirks and foibles of my character started to make sense."
