Spider-Man 4 In Serious Trouble Because Of Sony Relationship With Kevin Feige

Publish date: 2024-08-19

Tom Holland, who first put on the Spidey suit in the 2016 film Captain America: Civil War, went on to play the Stan Lee creation in the 2017 feature Spider-Man: Homecoming. A favorite among fans, Holland reprised this role in Avengers: Infinity War, Avengers: Endgame, Spider-Man: Far From Home, and the recent smash hit Spider-Man: No Way Home. So long as Spiderman 4 continues in development, Holland will be once again slinging webs and fighting bad guys.
