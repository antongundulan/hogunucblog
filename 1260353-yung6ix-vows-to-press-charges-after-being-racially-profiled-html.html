Yung6ix vows to press charges after being racially profiled in the US

Publish date: 2024-08-15

Nigerian rapper Onome Onokohwomo, popularly known by his stge name Yung6ix, has vowed to sue after he was racially profiled in the United States of America (USA).

In a video shared on his official Instagram page, the young man disclosed that he was just released from jail, after he was wrongfully accused of theft by a business owner he identified as Kay Jewellers.

He expressed that he spent an ample period of his time thinking if he would become the next black man to be mistreated by the system.

Yung6ix also revealed that he was racially profiled and he would be pressing charges.
