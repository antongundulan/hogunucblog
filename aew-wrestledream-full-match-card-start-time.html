AEW WrestleDream Full Match Card &amp; Start Time

Publish date: 2024-08-07

After AEW All In London, Tony Khan was pleased to announce that WrestleDream is coming to AEW, a tribute to Antonio Inoki. The show is taking place tonight and fans couldn’t be more excited for what Tony Khan has in store for them.

The AEW WrestleDream pay-per-view will be taking place on Sunday, October 1st from the Climate Pledge Arena in Seattle, WA. The pre-show will kick off at 7 PM ET while the main show will begin at 8 PM ET.

Christian Cage will be putting up his TNT Championship on the line against Darby Allin in the main event of AEW WrestleDream. The rumors about Edge going to AEW are huge right now as many believe he will debut at the end of WrestleDream this week.

The other highly anticipated match will see MJF defending the ROH Tag Team Championships against The Righteous in a handicap match. With Adam Cole out of action for the foreseeable future, the odds are certainly stacked against MJF.

Other title matches at the pay-per-view include Kris Statlander defending her TBS Championship against Julia Hart, Eddie Kingston defending the ROH & NJPW Strong Championships against Katsuyori Shibata and FTR putting their AEW World Tag Team Titles against Aussie Open.

All in all, the event boasts several matches for fans to enjoy. It remains to be seen whether the event will end up becoming a success, as AEW certainly needs a win right now. Keep checking back at Ringside News as we will provide play-by-play coverage of the AEW WrestleDream pay-per-view
