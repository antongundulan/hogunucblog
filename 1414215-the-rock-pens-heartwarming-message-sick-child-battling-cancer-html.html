The Rock Pens Heartwarming Message for Sick Child Battling Cancer

Publish date: 2024-08-31

- Dwayne 'The Rock' Johnson took time out of his evening to send a powerful message to a sick girl online

- The young girl is named Indy Llew and she's been battling cancer so The Rock sent her some encouragement

- Social media users admired just how far The Rock was willing to go to make the young girl's day

Dwayne 'The Rock' Johnson recently took to social media to send a lovely message to a sick child so he could make her day.

The Rock was just about to go to bed and thought that he could record something sweet for the beautiful child, named Indy Llew. He captioned his post:

"Stay strong honey and keep singing your songs! Love you, Maui."

The actor played Maui in the movie Moana and he included a video of the young girl battling cancer singing a song from the film.

The Rock sent her a positive message saying that she probably wouldn't recognise him but that's okay with him. He also went on to rap a sweet rhyme for Indy to make her feel better.
