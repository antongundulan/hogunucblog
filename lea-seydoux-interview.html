Actress La Seydoux Street Style

Publish date: 2024-08-22

But by her estimation, no one outranks her slightly anti-fashion mother in personal style. "It's not about the clothes for her," Seydoux says. "She wears no makeup except red lipstick, always. She has a beautiful mouth." And it's her father who dispensed the best style advice she's ever received, counseling his daughter to behave like a beautiful girl. "You have to make others believe that you are," she explains. That's a role that requires no effort from Seydoux, whose beauty needs little window dressing, making her timeless wardrobe a spot-on complement. Though she's gaining recognition stateside with her American films, don't expect her trend-immune tendency to change. "It's very different in Paris," she observes. "In New York, they are more rock 'n' roll. There are more tattoos and piercings. In Paris, it's more classic, you know?" She concludes with a giggle. "And we smoke."
