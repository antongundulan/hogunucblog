Michael Giacchino (Italian pronunciation: [dakkino]; born October 10, 1967) is an American composer who has written music for films, television shows, and video games. He has won many awards for his work. The scores to television shows such as Lost, Alias, and Fringe, video games such as the Medal of Honor and Call of Duty series, and films such as Mission: Impossible III, The Incredibles, Star Trek: Into Darkness, Cloverfield, Ratatouille, Super 8, Cars 2, 50/50 and Dawn of the Planet of the Apes are some of his most well-known works. The composer has won many honours for his work, including an Emmy, multiple Grammys, a Golden Globe Award, and an Academy Award, among others.
