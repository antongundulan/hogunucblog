REPORT: ALTAR-REFLECT - Destiny 2 Guide

Publish date: 2024-08-09

This section of IGN's Destiny 2: The Witch Queen guide covers the REPORT: ALTAR-REFLECT Quest which can be retrieved from the Evidence Board. This page will cover how to complete the REPORT: ALTAR-REFLECT Quest as well as detail the rewards you'll receive.

advertisement

How to Complete REPORT: ALTAR-REFLECT

This Quest can be acquired from the Evidence Board which is located nearby in The Enclave on Mars. Interact with it to complete Step 1.

Step 2: Visit the Altar of Reflection in the Temple of the Wrathful

Head to the Throne World and complete the Altar of Reflection: Catalyst mission. You'll find this mission located inside the Temple of the Wrathful. Clicking the icon on your map will lead you to the destination.

When you arrive, interact with the Deepsight orb and the glowing Witch Queen symbol will appear on the wall. Head to the opposite corner of the room across from the glowing symbol and alight yourself so it creates the logo. Next, jump inside the portal to be transported back to the Altar of Reflection.

How to Solve the Rune Puzzle

advertisement

When you arrive at the Altar of Reflection, you'll be greeted by a familiar Rune puzzle that needs to be solved. This is the exact same as the Memories of Loss mission from The Witch Queen Campaign, so you should have it down by now.

As a reminder, the key to solving the Rune Patterns puzzles is to look for a pattern and then locate the runes that don't follow the pattern. Runes are arranged in groups of five, viewed vertically. The first puzzle features eight columns, with three columns having incorrectly arranged runes. Shoot the column with the incorrect set of runes and a large glowing Hive rune will spawn in the air above, indicating a correct answer. If you happen to shoot the wrong rune, the message "Incorrect rune chosen. The trial begins again" will appear to the left of your screen and you'll need to start over.

The second set of puzzles (as introduced in the Memories of Loss Quest) adds more columns for you to decipher. The rules remain the same, though, as you're still looking for the three columns that don't match the rest of the bunch. The runes are in groups of three located around the circular structures in the area.

After the puzzles have been solved, head into the doorway to face the Lucent Archivists. This time, you'll be up against the Witch's Eye, Keeper of Memory - a Hive Shrieker enemy. This is probably the toughest enemy you've faced in the Altar of Reflection thus far simply due to the amount of firepower it can lay down if you remain out of cover for too long.

Once the enemies have been defeated, head into the final room and view the memory to complete this Quest step.

Step 3 & 4: Log the evidence and review the case on the Evidence Board in the Enclave.

Return to the Evidence Board in the Enclave to log the data and receive your reward, a Throne World Legendary weapon.
