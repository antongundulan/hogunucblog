Shotzi Blackheart &amp; Brandi Lauren Declared A Better Couple Than Sammy Guevara &amp; Tay Conti

Publish date: 2024-08-27

Sammy Guevara and Tay Conti were initially beloved by fans when they were not together on AEW television. After becoming couple, fans simply got tired of seeing them together on AEW television and their PDA sessions on social media didn’t help matters. Simps were pissed and Guevara was no longer their Spanish God. Now Brandi Lauren has added her two cents on the matter.

Sammy Guevara won the TNT Championship during AEW Battle Of The Belts this past week. Right after that, the two celebrated with a photo. Interestingly enough, Sammy Guevara turned off comments for that particular post.

Ringside News recently made a post where we asked fans to name a better couple than Sammy Guevara and Tay Conti. Yes, this was a sarcastic question, but it received a ton of replies.

Brandi Lauren replied to the tweet, as she threw a tiny bit of shade at the two AEW stars by uploading a photo of herself with Shotzi Blackheart.

No hate but we can’t be beat

With Sammy Guevara and Tay Conti being heels now. it remains to be seen whether AEW will properly capitalize on the opportunity or not.

No, Shotzi and Brandi are not an actual couple, and Ringside News killed the rumor that Blackheart got heat in WWE for faking lesbian relationship. That doesn’t stop them from being a better couple than Conti and Guevara.
