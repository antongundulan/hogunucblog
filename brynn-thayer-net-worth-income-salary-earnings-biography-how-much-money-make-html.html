Ms. Thayer (born October 4, 1949) is an American actress who has been in many television shows, most notably Matlock, where she played Matlock’s daughter, Leanne MacIntyre. She is also known for her appearance in the film The Last of the Mohicans. She attends the Beverly Hills Playhouse acting school, where she learns acting. Thayer was born in North Dallas, Texas, to Margery (Schwartz) and William Paul Thayer, a former navy officer and business entrepreneur who served as Deputy Secretary of Defense in the Reagan Administration from 1983 to 198Thayer attended North Dallas High School. The charity foundation ZazAngels, which she co-founded with actor Michael Zaslow and his wife, Susan Hufford, was established to raise money for research into Amyotrophic Lateral Sclerosis (ALS), commonly known as Lou Gehrig’s illness or Lou Gehrig’s disease.
