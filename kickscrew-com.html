How much does shipping cost?

How can I get a first order discount at KicksCrew?

The simplest way to get a discount on your first order is by shopping with a KicksCrew promo code. You can also get $10 off your first order when referred by a friend to KicksCrew. Once you complete your order, the referee will also get a $10 voucher.  

What payment methods are available at KicksCrew?

KicksCrew accepts a wide range of payment methods such as most major debit and credit cards. These include Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover (to name a few). The brand also accepts more modern payments such as Apple Pay, Google Pay, and PayPal.  

How do I contact KicksCrew?

If you need to contact KicksCrew, simply head over to the KicksCrew website, scroll to the bottom of the homepage, and press the ‘Contact Us’ button. Then fill in the required information and hit the ‘Submit’ button.   

What is the returns policy at KicksCrew?

KicksCrew has a 7 day returns policy from the date of delivery. If you choose to return an item, you will only receive a partial refund due to restocking fees. The original delivery cost will not be refunded. It is also the customer’s responsibility to cover the return shipping costs. Any returns should have tags still attached and be in their original, perfect condition.
