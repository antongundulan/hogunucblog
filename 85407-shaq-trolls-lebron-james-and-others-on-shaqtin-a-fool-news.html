Shaq Trolls LeBron James &amp; Others On &quot;Shaqtin' A Fool&quot;

Publish date: 2024-08-14

Shaquille O'Neal unleashed a brand new episode of "Shaqtin' A Fool" last night, as is tradition for the NBA on TNT's Thursday night doubleheader.

In the weekly recap of NBA bloopers, Shaq, Charles Barkley, Kenny "The Jet" Smith, Ernie Johnson and the viewers at home witnessed LeBron James making a rare appearance as what Shaq described as, 'Tragic Bronson.'

LeBron's ridiculous dribbling exhibition against Chicago's rookie Lauri Markkanen led directly to a turnover and ultimately landed him on the Shaqtin A Fool lowlight reel. Despite the rare on-court folly, LeBron and the Cavs picked up a win against the Bulls and are currently riding a 13-game winning streak as they head into tonight's matchup against the Pacers in Indiana. 

Also in this episode of Shaqtin', we saw recurring guest Marcus Smart as well as Houston's Zhou Qi, Golden Sate's Zaza Pachulia and Nicholas Batum, who may have prevented Miami's Josh Richardson from ever having children.
