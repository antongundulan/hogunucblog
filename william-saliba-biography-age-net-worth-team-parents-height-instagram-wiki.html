Career

After starting his journey with the youth academy of AS Saint-Étienne, this talented individual successfully made his professional debut in September 2018. 

Demonstrating remarkable skills and dedication, he rapidly secured a spot on the Saint-Étienne first team, becoming a consistent player on the field. Not only did he impress with his performances, but he also received recognition for his outstanding abilities by being awarded the prestigious title of Ligue 1 Young Player of the Year in 2019.

In July 2019, William Saliba completed a £27 million transfer to Arsenal. However, instead of joining the club immediately, he was loaned back to Saint-Étienne for the 2019–20 season. 

After spending a year on loan, Saliba finally arrived at Arsenal in July 2020. However, his time with the club was short-lived, as he was once again sent out on loan for the 2020–21 season, this time to Nice.
