Prince Harry and Meghan Markle reunite in Jamaica

Publish date: 2024-08-07

    Prince Harry and Meghan Markle have taken a romantic holiday together to Jamaica! The loved-up couple flew to Montego Bay on Wednesday to attend the wedding of one of the fifth-in-line to the throne's best friends – Tom 'Skippy' Inskip. According to the , the Prince enjoyed a Virgin Atlantic flight in premium economy, while Meghan flew from Toronto to meet him there in a private jet. Tom will be getting married to literary agent Lara Hughes-Young, and a friend told the newspaper that the three-day lavish event will be "the mother of all parties," with celebrities including Usain Bolt rumoured to make an appearance to congratulate the newly wedded couple. Tom and Lara have reportedly hired out the Round Hill Hotel and Villas, which includes an infinity pool, spa and hotel suites designed by Ralph Lauren.

    This isn't the first holiday the pair have taken together, as they also enjoyed a trip to the town of Tromso in Norway at the beginning of January. During their stay, the couple stayed in a cabin, took part in whale watching and watch the Northern Lights. Although the pair were apart over Christmas, they also spent time together in December when the actress stayed with the Prince at Kensington Palace. They were spotted attending and buying a Christmas tree together.
