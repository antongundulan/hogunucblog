Matthew Perry is an American TV actor best known for his role as Chandler Bing on the NBC sitcom Friends. He has also starred in several films, such as Fools Rush In and The Whole Nine Yards, and has had lead roles in several short-lived TV series, including Mr. Sunshine, Go On, The Odd Couple, and Studio 60 on the Sunset Strip. Perry was born on August 19, 1969 in Williamstown, MA.

About

TV actor who became known as the wisecracking Chandler Bing on the NBC sitcom Friends. He also played lead roles in the films Fools Rush In and The Whole Nine Yards and starred in the short-lived TV series Mr. Sunshine, Go On, The Odd Couple and Studio 60 on the Sunset Strip. 

Before Fame

He was a top-ranked junior tennis player in Canada as a teenager.

Trivia

In 2006, he received Emmy and Golden Globe nominations for his leading role in the TV movie The Ron Clark Story. He played the grown up version of Zac Efron's character in the 2009 film 17 Again.

Family Life

He dated Actress Lizzy Caplan from 2006 to 2012. He previously dated Julia Roberts and Lauren Graham. 

Associated With

He starred in the popular sitcom Friends alongside Jennifer Aniston, David Schwimmer, Matt LeBlanc, Lisa Kudrow and Courteney Cox from 1994 to 2004.
