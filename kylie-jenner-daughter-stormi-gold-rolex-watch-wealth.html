KYLIE Jenner has been criticized for flaunting her luxury goods in a new video.

The Hulu star went through the items in her bag on TikTok, but one expensive piece owned by her daughter Stormi was slammed by fans.

In the video, Kylie sat in one of her cars and went through her high-fashion Bottega bag, which she lauded as the best investment.

The 25-year-old wore a white tank top to show off her recent weight loss, and minimal makeup with plenty of silver jewelry.

After running through some cosmetics products from her own Kylie Skin range, Kylie pulled a stunning gold watch out of the bag.

She explained: "I have Stormi's little watch in here. It was actually my watch but she wore it - look how small her wrist is - she wore it for a birthday party and didn't want to wear it anymore."
