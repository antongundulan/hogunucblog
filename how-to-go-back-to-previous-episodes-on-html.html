How To Go Back To Previous Episodes On Hulu

Publish date: 2024-08-14
People are currently reading this guide.

Uh Oh! Did Hulu Swallow Your Previous Episode? Fear Not, We Can Fish It Out!

So you're all comfy, popcorn in hand, ready to dive back into that show you've been binging. But wait... where'd the earlier episodes go? Did Hulu develop a sudden taste for chronology and devour them whole? Don't panic! Retrieving those missing episodes is easier than remembering your high school locker combination (hopefully easier...).

Navigating the Hulu Labyrinth: A Guide for Time-Traveling Viewers

Here's your handy guide to become a Hulu Indiana Jones, unearthing hidden episodes and vanquishing the evil forces of confusion:

Method 1: The Show Page Shuffle

  • Channel Your Inner Bloodhound: Sniff out that show's page on Hulu. You can usually find it by searching for the show's name or browsing through categories (Mystery? Maybe that's where the missing episodes went...).
  • Behold! The Glorious Poster: Once you're on the show's page, feast your eyes on that glorious poster (because, let's face it, sometimes the poster is the best part). Right below it, you should see a glorious list of episodes.
  • Time Travel with Arrows: Use those nifty left and right arrow buttons (or your fancy scrolling skills) to unearth those hidden gems – the previous episodes!

    • Method 2: The "Keep Watching" Cavern

  • Unearth the Forgotten Path: Head back to the glorious Hulu homepage. Cast your gaze upon the mystical "Keep Watching" section. This is where Hulu stashes shows you've recently embarked on adventures with.
  • The Show Must Go On (But Maybe a Few Episodes Back): Find your show lurking in the "Keep Watching" shadows. Click on that bad boy, and voila! A list of previous episodes should magically appear, ready to be re-watched.

    • Bonus Tip: The Power of Search

    Still stuck? Don't despair! Type in the specific episode number and title you seek in the Hulu search bar. This magic key might just unlock the episode and send you hurtling back into the show's delightful chaos.

    There you have it! With these simple steps, you'll be a Hulu episode retrieval master in no time. Now go forth, reclaim your lost viewing time, and remember – happy streaming!
