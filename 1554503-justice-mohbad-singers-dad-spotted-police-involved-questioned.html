Justice for Mohbad: Singers Dad Spotted As Police Say Everyone Involved Would Be Questioned

Publish date: 2024-08-16
  • The Lagos state police recently held a press conference following the death of singer Mohbad and cries for justice to prevail
  • Trending videos from the press conference showed singer Mohbad's dad was also present
  • The police revealed that everyone involved in Mohbad's case would be questioned, stirring reactions among many Nigerian music lovers

Amid the call for justice over singer Mohbad's death by many Nigerians, including celebrities like Tonto Dikeh, and Iyabo Ojo, among others, the Lagos state commissioner of police, Idowu Owohunwa, in a press release on Monday, September 18, revealed everyone involved in the case would be brought in for questioning.

The commissioner assured the public, saying everyone whose name was mentioned would not escape anywhere on earth.

The police also called on anyone with helpful information to provide them for investigation.
