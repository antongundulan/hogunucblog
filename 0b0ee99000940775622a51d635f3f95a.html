WHY IS STEPPING SUCH A GREAT EXERCISE?

“Stepping is a brilliant exercise as it works your glutes, hamstrings and core mainly. If you step quickly it is also a great cardio workout,” explains Ben.

To get the best workout from a stepper machine, Ben recommends two different methods.

“If you are stepping at a moderate pace you will need to train longer to get results, so for 45-60 minutes. Or you can do interval training, where you go all-out for a minute (for example) and then reduce the intensity for 20 seconds. After 20 minutes of that you will feel a burn.”

STEPPER MACHINE VS STAIR CLIMBER MACHINE

When deciding what sort of stepper machine to invest in, you’re going to want to think about your budget and how much space you have in your home.

Generally speaking, stepper machines are more affordable and easier to fit into your home. A stair climber machine is almost always more expensive.

Ben Lucas says “a stair climber will work you harder as it mimics real life, but it does take up a lot of space so you may be better off getting a stepper that you can use in front of the TV (for example), and then just walk or run up the steps to mimic a stair climber.”
