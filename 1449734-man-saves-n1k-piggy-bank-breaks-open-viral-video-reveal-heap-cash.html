Reactions as Man Breaks His Piggy Bank After 1 Year, Reveals Heap of Cash

Publish date: 2024-08-25
  • A man broke his piggy bank where he had been saving for the past year to reveal massive N1000 notes
  • In a viral video, the man packed crumpled naira notes out as if he were bringing out pieces of papers
  • Nigerians were amazed by his determination to save that much, as some said that they did not have such patience

A video showing a Nigerian man, Prince Chinedu, packing crumpled notes of N1,000 into a messy heap on the floor has stirred reactions.

In the clip, the man dug into a wooden box, conversing with someone behind the camera. He took the money out as if it were some trash.

Another Nigerian man broke his piggy bank

Meanwhile, Legit.ng earlier reported that a Nigerian man with the name Emmanuel Olatunji Ega stirred massive reactions online as he showed off the reward of his savings habit.

In a Facebook post on Thursday, December 2, he shared a photo of a piggy bank he just broke. Around the wooden receptacle are different notes that comprised mostly of N1,000 and N500.

He revealed that seeing all the money he had saved up makes him feel like the richest man in the world.
