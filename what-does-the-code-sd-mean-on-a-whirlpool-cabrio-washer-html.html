What does the code SD mean on a Whirlpool Cabrio washer?

Publish date: 2024-08-16

Answer

On the display of your Whirlpool Washer, you will see “Sud” or “Sd.”

This might be caused by the use of excessive detergent or the failure to use High Efficiency (HE) detergent. The washer will not be able to spin off water because of the surplus suds that has accumulated. When the words “Sud” or “Sd” are shown on the washer’s display, the washer automatically increases the rinse time to eliminate the excess suds.

 The F/dL error number on the machine indicates that the door lock has failed. As a result, what is the best way to get rid of extra suds in the washing machine?

If your washer has completed the rinse cycle and you open the door to discover suds still within, add 1/2 cup of vinegar to 1 quart of water in the washing machine and set the machine to “rinse.” The vinegar will remove any lingering suds from the washer and clothing, ensuring that your laundry is clean when it is finished.
