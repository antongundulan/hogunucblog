Ryan Nemeth Seemingly Hints At Dolph Ziggler Joining AEW

Publish date: 2024-08-19

Dolph Ziggler has been a mainstay on WWE television since signing with the company in 2004. The Showoff has accomplished a lot in WWE but that doesn’t mean he is done with the company. That said, it appears his brother Ryan Nemeth seemingly hinted at Ziggler joining AEW.

Dolph Ziggler is a two-time World Heavyweight Champion, having won the title back in 2011 and 2013 respectively. Unfortunately, he was never seen in the main event title picture after 2013, much to the dismay of fans. In recent memory, he had shorter run with the NXT Title.

Dolph Ziggler’s brother Ryan Nemeth took to Twitter and uploaded a photo of himself and Ziggler, where they were seen holding the AEW World Championship, TNT Championship and AEW World Tag Team Championships. He added a clock emoji, seemingly hinting that Ziggler’s AEW jump is simply a matter of time.

Dolph Ziggler also made an appearance on Being The Elite last month. Ziggler hasn’t been seen on WWE television since facing JD McDonagh on the May 29 edition of Monday Night Raw. We’ll have to wait and see whether Ziggler will end up joining his brother in AEW somewhere down the line.
