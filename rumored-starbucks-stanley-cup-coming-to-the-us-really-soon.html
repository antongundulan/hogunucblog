Rumored Starbucks Stanley cup coming to the US really soon

Publish date: 2024-08-29

Everyone is going gaga for a Starbucks Stanley cup that launched in Thailand – and it looks like one could be coming to the US very soon.

The Stanley Cup, an insulated metal flask with a handle and straw, went viral on TikTok earlier in 2023 and is the latest must-have social media product.

Everyone is obsessed with the cute pastel colors and the 40 oz tumbler, which retails for $40, is always sold out and often resells for more than $100.

Starbucks has been teaming up with the drinkware brand for years, but a new turquoise handle cup that was released in Thailand sent the internet wild.

Everyone was gutted the cup was only available in Asia, but some rumors about a new US release are now flooding TikTok…

Rumored US Starbucks Stanley cup

Pictures of a Starbucks x Stanley cup with a handle began emerging on the video app at the start of April which many claim is coming to the US.

The 40 oz cup and is an adorable baby pink color with a transparent lid and straw and has a rumored price of $44.95.

It hasn’t been confirmed by the coffee chain when writing this, but rumors say the cup is arriving with the Chapter 1 release on May 9.

HITC and GRV Media have reached out to Starbucks for comment.

TikTok users are going crazy

One TikTok user called Rayah Sunshine who shares new Starbucks releases posted photos of the Stanley cup and her video has gone viral.

Everyone is going crazy in the comments, where one person wrote: “Going to all the Starbucks stores within a 100 mile radius.”

Another said: “This is going to sell out so quick! I want one.”

“Best believe I’m buying this with my discount,” a Starbucks employee added.

Others are concerned that they won’t be able to get their hands on one at all because people will buy them all and resell them for extortionate prices.

“These resellers abut to be charging 1k for this,” someone else wrote.

Another commented: “They need to put a limit on one person person so others have a chance.”

Grab a bottle of Starbucks’ Pink Drink

While you wait for the rumored Starbucks Stanley collab to arrive in the US, run to stores to get your hands on another exciting new product.

Starbucks has launched its famous Pink Drink in a bottle and it’s hitting grocery store shelves across America this week.

It will be available at all stores where groceries are sold alongside a bottled version of the much-loved Paradise Drink too.

The Pink Drink has bold fruit flavors of strawberry and açaí combined with coconut milk and refreshing fruit juice and is a firm favorite.

Starbucks’ new ready-to-drink bottles will have a suggested retail price of $3.67 although prices will vary by retailer.
