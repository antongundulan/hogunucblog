Rhea Ripley Approves of Edited Image Showcasing Her Backside in LA Knights Glasses

Publish date: 2024-08-24

The internet wrestling community is full of funny images, and photoshopped mastery. There are also a lot of fans who pay attention to the more titillating aspects of sports entertainment, and Rhea Ripley seems to be all about that.

A photo of WWE’s Judgment Day faction celebrating a victory gained widespread attention on social media. Then fans noticed one very prominent aspect of the photograph.

Based on this viral photo, someone generated a photoshopped image where the image of Rhea Ripley backside’s reflection appeared in LA Knight’s sunglasses.

Rhea Ripley saw this, and she obviously wasn’t offended. The WWE Women’s World Champion chimed in to acknowledge the image, using Knight’s “YEAH!” catchphrase.

We will have to see what Rhea Ripley comments on next, because there are a lot of photos of her floating around out there. It seems that she is very proud of herself, as she should be, so fans may have a green light to photo shop to their heart’s content.
